# Hong Kong Drug Registration Guide

## Overview

Hong Kong's drug regulatory system is overseen by the **Pharmacy and Poisons Board of Hong Kong (PPBH)** under the Pharmacy and Poisons Ordinance (Cap. 138). The **Drug Office** of the Department of Health handles day-to-day administration of pharmaceutical regulation.

## Official Drug Database

### Search Drug Database

**URL**: https://www.drugoffice.gov.hk/eps/do/en/consumer/search_drug_database2.html

#### Search Fields

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| HK Registration No. | Format: HK-XXXXX | HK-12345 |
| Product/Drug Name | Brand name | Keytruda |
| Active Ingredient | Chemical name | Pembrolizumab |
| Certificate Holder | Manufacturer/Distributor | Merck Sharp & Dohme |

#### Search Options

- **Match all**: All search terms must match
- **Match either one**: Any term matches
- Results per page: 20, 40, 60, 80, 100, 500, 1000

### List of Registered Pharmaceutical Products

Available at: https://www.drugoffice.gov.hk/eps/do/en/consumer/reg_pharm_products/index.html

Products can be downloaded in XML format (English only) from data.gov.hk.

## Hong Kong Drug Registration Number Format

Format: **HK-XXXXX** (e.g., HK-12345)

The registration number is required for importing and dispensing any pharmaceutical product in Hong Kong.

## Drug Classification in Hong Kong

### By Prescription Requirement

| Classification | Abbreviation | Description |
|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| Prescription Only Medicines | PoM | Requires doctor's prescription |
| Pharmacy Only Medicines | P | Sold at registered pharmacies with pharmacist |
| Over The Counter Medicines | OTC | Can be sold at any licensed premises |

### By Legal Category

- **Part I**: poisons for therapeutic use
- **Part II**: dangerous poisons
- **Part III**: controlled drugs

## The "1+" Mechanism

Hong Kong implemented a new **"1+" mechanism** for new drug approval:

- **Effective Date**: November 1, 2024
- **Previous Requirement**: TwoPhase I trials from recognized institutions
- **New Requirement**: ONE Phase I trial from recognized institution + primary evaluation in Hong Kong
- **Scope**: All new chemical or biological entities
- **Benefit**: Accelerates approval of innovative drugs for severe or rare diseases

## Electronic Certificate of Drug/Product Registration

- **Launched**: July 1, 2023
- **Format**: Electronic certificate replaces paper certificates
- **Verification**: Available through Drug Office verification system

## Drug Dealer Verification

To verify if a pharmacy or medicine store is licensed:

**URL**: https://www.drugoffice.gov.hk/eps/do/en/consumer/search_drug_dealers2.html

| License Type | Description | Allowed to Sell |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Authorized Seller of Poisons (ASP) | Pharmacy with pharmacist | Prescription Only Medicines, Pharmacy Only, OTC |
| Listed Seller of Poisons (LSP) | Medicine store | OTC medicines only |

## Import/Export Requirements

- Import license required for pharmaceutical products
- Export license needed for certain controlled substances
- Application through Drug Office
