# Hong Kong Major Cancer Centers

## Overview

Hong Kong's healthcare system includes both public and private hospitals. For cancer treatment, several institutions are renowned for their oncology services, research capabilities, and specialized cancer centers.

### 3. 玛丽医院 (Queen Mary Hospital) - Public

**Location**: 102 Pokfulam Road, Hong Kong

**Type**: Public Hospital / Teaching Hospital of University of Hong Kong

**Notes**:
- Major tertiary referral center
- Comprehensive cancer treatment services
- Heavily subsidized fees for Hong Kong residents
- Long waiting times for non-emergency cases (10+ weeks for specialist referrals)

## Cross-Border Considerations

### For Mainland Patients Seeking Treatment in HK

1. **Documentation Required**
   - Valid travel documents (Mainland Travel Permit)
   - Medical records and reports
   - Pathology slides and blocks
   - Imaging films/CDs

2. **Payment**
   - Most private hospitals require advance deposit
   - International patients pay market rates (not subsidized)
   - Credit cards accepted (Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay)

3. **Follow-up Care**
   - Coordination with mainland physicians possible
   - eHealth app allows cross-boundary health record sharing (since July 2024)

### For HK Residents Seeking Treatment on Mainland

See cross-border-policy.md for:
- Elderly Health Care Voucher GBA Pilot Scheme
- Hospital Authority GBA Patient Support Scheme
- Direct settlement arrangements
