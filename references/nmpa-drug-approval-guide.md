# NMPA Drug Approval Database Guide

## Overview

The **National Medical Products Administration (NMPA)** is China's primary regulatory body for pharmaceutical products, medical devices, and cosmetics. It was formerly known as the China Food and Drug Administration (CFDA) until its restructuring in 2018.

## How to Check Drug Approval Status

### Official NMPA Website

**URL**: https://www.nmpa.gov.cn/

1. Navigate to the NMPA homepage
2. Click on "药品" (Drugs) section
3. Select "药品查询" (Drug Search)
4. Enter the drug name, active ingredient, or approval number

### Key Search Parameters

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| 批准文号 | Approval number | 国药准字H20190001 |
| 商品名 | Brand name | 可瑞达 |
| 通用名 | Generic name | 帕博利珠单抗 |
| 生产企业 | Manufacturer | 默沙东 |

### Drug Approval Number Format

Chinese drug approval numbers follow this format:
- **H** + 8 digits: Chemical drugs (H = 化学药品)
- **S** + 8 digits: Biologics (S = 生物制品)
- **Z** + 8 digits: Traditional Chinese Medicine (Z = 中药)
- **J** + 8 digits: Medical devices (J = 进口药品)

## Key Regulatory Pathways

### 1. Standard Review
- Standard timeline for generic drugs
- Typically 6-12 months for approval

### 2. Priority Review
- For innovative drugs addressing unmet medical needs
- Timeline shortened to 3-6 months
- Criteria: national priority items, AIDS drugs, pediatric drugs

### 3. Conditional Approval
- For drugs with urgent clinical need
- Requires post-marketing surveillance
- Common for oncology drugs

### 4. Breakthrough Therapy Designation
- For drugs demonstrating substantial improvement over existing therapies
- Enhanced guidance during development

## Critical Notes for International Patients

1. **NMPA approval does NOT guarantee immediate availability**
   - Priority-review drugs may only be dispensed at trial-participating hospitals for 6-12 months
   - International patients must verify target hospital is on approved dispensing list

2. **Pricing and Reimbursement**
   - NHSA (National Healthcare Security Administration) negotiations take 3-6 months after NMPA approval
   - International patients pay list price, not negotiated NHSA price
   - Expect NRL inclusion 90+ days after full approval

## NMPA Drug Safety Alerts

The NMPA publishes safety alerts and recall information regularly. Check:
- 不良反应通报 (Adverse Reaction Reports)
- 药品召回信息 (Drug Recall Information)
- 质量公告 (Quality Announcements)

## Regional Considerations

For drugs approved in China, availability may vary by province due to:
- Provincial pharmaceutical procurement processes
- Hospital pharmacy formulary decisions
- Local reimbursement policies

International patients should consult with Tier-3A hospitals directly for specific drug access.
